OmniSight is a simple, single-glance monitoring dashboard for Proxmox, Linux servers, Kubernetes, SNMP devices, Docker, databases, Healthchecks, Uptime Kuma and Prometheus.

Stack: Node.js + Express backend, single-file vanilla HTML/CSS/JS frontend. Alerts via ntfy, Telegram and SMTP.
