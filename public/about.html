OmniSight is a simple, single-glance monitoring dashboard for Proxmox, Linux servers, Kubernetes, Synology (SNMP), Docker and Healthchecks.

Stack: Node.js + Express backend, single-file vanilla HTML/CSS/JS frontend. Alerts via ntfy, Telegram and SMTP.
